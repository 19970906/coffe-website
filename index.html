about us

what makes our coffee special?

Coffee sources 100% of its beans locally from over 300 farming families across the luscious highland forests of Sri Lanka, in the hill country of regions Ella, Welimada and Kotmale. The experience of our farmers, along with their heart and soul, is what waters this phenomenal shrub to its full potential.

Coffee proudly collaborates with these hardworking and extremely skilled individuals to grow our premium quality coffee beans, without using any environment-devastating mono-culture practices, and following ethical farming standards.Soul Coffee provides direct assistance to the farmers by providing coffee plants, fertilizer, machinery and guaranteed premium price for their harvest.

At Coffee, the ethically conscious approach to present you the perfect cup of coffee that you can enjoy without any reservations, is something we strongly believe in and reflects our core values.
